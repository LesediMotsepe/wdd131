Stationery Hub Site Plan

Site Name

The site name is "Stationery Hub".

Site Purpose

The purpose of Stationery Hub is to provide a one-stop online shop for students to purchase stationery and school supplies.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

Emily, a high school student, needs to purchase school supplies for the upcoming semester.

Scenario 2:

David, a college student, needs to purchase specialized art supplies for his graphic design course.

Color Schema

The color schema for Stationery Hub consists of two main colors:

  • Primary color: #3498db (a bright, calming blue)
  • Secondary color: #f1c40f (a warm, vibrant yellow)

Typography

The typography for Stationery Hub consists of two main fonts:

  • Open Sans (or a similar sans-serif font) for the main body text
  • Montserrat (or a similar geometric sans-serif font) for headings and titles
